About Me

I am a high school student from the Bothell area to change and inspire the residents of the city through a series of volunteering initiatives and impactful public speaking engagements. My name is Venkat, and I firmly believe in the power of community service and the ability of words to ignite positive change. These experiences shaped my perspective on the world and ignited a deep passion for making a difference. Determined to spread the same spirit of kindness and empathy, I embarked on a journey of volunteering, aiming to touch as many lives as possible.
